 **Creating a Comprehensive History of Knives**


Knives are one of the oldest and most important tools known to mankind. Throughout history, knives have been used for everything from hunting and self-defense to cooking and crafting. This paper will take a comprehensive look at the history of knives and how they have evolved over time. 

The earliest evidence of knife use dates back to the Paleolithic era, between 10,000 and 400,000 BC. These early knives were usually made from chipped stone and used for cutting, hunting, and preparing food.
