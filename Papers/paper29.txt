 

For the purpose of this paper, the history of Mac Trucks will be explored from its beginnings until present day. 

Mac Trucks originated in 1908 in Gary, Indiana when the company was founded by Obert C. Martin and named after his initials, forming the name “O.C. Mac Truck Company”. The original Mac Trucks included a variety of larger trucks and were driven as one-ton, two-ton, five-ton and seven-ton vehicles. The earliest models used solid rubber tires with steel rims and chain drive to the rear axle.
